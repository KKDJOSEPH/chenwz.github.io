Projects

Project 1: Evaluation of the Effect of Information Technology on Student Development

Nov 2015 - Dec 2017
  • Evaluated the effect of information technology on the development of 770 elementary students from 23 schools in Xian’an City, Hubei Province.
  • Designed evaluation tools for student development and technology acceptance.
  • Verified that technical intervention has a significant positive impact on the academics, psychology, and behavioral development of students in rural schools by sharing resources of schools in developed areas with rural small-sized schools using distant real-time interactive video.
